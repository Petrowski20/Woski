# Product Vision

## Purpose

Woski exists to modernize the way groups of friends experience prediction games.

What started as a personal Excel spreadsheet used to manage a football prediction league has evolved into the vision of a platform where anyone can create private competitions, make predictions and enjoy tournaments together.

Woski is not about gambling.

It is about bringing people together through friendly competition.

---

## Origin Story

The project was born from a real problem.

For years, predictions were managed manually using an Excel spreadsheet.

Friends submitted their predictions through WhatsApp messages and polls.

After every match someone had to:

- Collect every prediction.
- Update the spreadsheet.
- Calculate scores manually.
- Update the ranking.
- Share the results.

As the competitions grew, maintaining the spreadsheet became increasingly time consuming.

Woski was created to automate that entire process while preserving what made it enjoyable: competing with friends.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional prediction games usually rely on spreadsheets, chat groups and manual calculations.

This approach creates unnecessary work and limits the experience.

Common problems include:

- Manual score calculations.
- Outdated rankings.
- Human errors.
- Difficult administration.
- Poor statistics.
- No flexibility for different competitions.

---

## Our Solution

Woski provides a platform where prediction games are automatic, flexible and social.

Users can create or join leagues, make predictions, follow rankings in real time and customize scoring systems depending on the competition.

The platform is designed to support sports and esports while remaining independent from any specific tournament.

---

## Target Audience

Woski is designed for groups of friends who enjoy following sports or esports competitions together.

Typical users are:

- Football fans.
- League of Legends fans.
- VALORANT fans.
- Communities.
- Companies.
- Student groups.

Anyone who enjoys friendly competition without gambling.

---

## Core Values

- Competition over gambling.
- Friends first.
- Fair play.
- Flexible by design.
- Open to everyone.
- Privacy when desired.
- Community driven.

---

## Product Pillars

Every feature in Woski should reinforce at least one of these pillars.

- Predictions.
- Private and public leagues.
- Live rankings.
- Configurable scoring rules.
- Statistics and historical data.
- Competition information.
- Team standings and simulations.

---

## Long-term Vision

We want Woski to become the natural place where groups organize prediction competitions.

Our goal is simple:

When a major tournament begins, people should naturally think:

> "Let's create a Woski."

---

## What Woski is NOT

Woski is not a betting platform.

Woski does not involve real money.

Woski does not promote gambling.

Woski is not designed around monetizing addiction.

Winning means bragging rights, friendly rivalry or perhaps a dinner paid by your friends—but never money inside the platform.

---

## Vision Statement

> Woski transforms traditional prediction games into a modern, social and customizable experience where friends compete together—not against the house.
